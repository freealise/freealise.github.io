local_atm Accept sponsorship from your audience

You need to have a Stripe payment provider account and live in a supported country or register a business there, which is easy to do with Stripe Atlas. In your account, add a one-time and a recurring product, each of them worth $1, and call them "one-time support" and "sponsorship" or anything like that. Into the form that generates the widget code you have to copy their unique IDs that begin with "sku_" and "plan_", as well as page addresses on your site for successful and cancelled payment, also fill in the necessary amount, suggested contribution, starting point which is the funds you already have (as you don't want to start at zero), and purpose of the payments. Make a new Google Apps Script project and copy this code into it, then click Publish and Deploy as web app with "Execute the app as" set to "Me" and access to "Anyone, even anonymous", copy the current web app URL and paste it into the form.
