--- layout: default title: "Spectral audio" link: /assets/images/v_ans.jpg caption: Virtual ANS interface credit: "Alexander Zolotov - 12 apr 2020" date: 2007-06-26 00:00:00 +0300 categories: jekyll update tags: new post --- Virtual ANS is a software simulator of the unique Russian synthesizer ANS - photoelectronic musical instrument created by Evgeny Murzin from 1938 to 1958. The ANS made it possible to draw music in the form of a spectrogram, without live instruments and performers.
